How many days to spend in Rome

Rome, the Eternal City, is a treasure trove of history and culture. To fully experience Rome, we recommend spending 4-6 days.

With 4-6 days, you can visit the Colosseum, explore the Vatican, stroll through the Roman Forum, and toss a coin into the Trevi Fountain. Add a day to explore Trastevere for a taste of local life.

Suggested Itinerary

  • Day 1: Visit the Colosseum and Roman Forum.
  • Day 2: Explore Vatican City and St. Peter’s Basilica.
  • Day 3: Stroll to the Trevi Fountain and Spanish Steps.
