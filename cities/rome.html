How Many Days to Spend in Rome

Rome, a city steeped in history, offers ancient ruins and vibrant culture. We recommend spending 4-6 days to explore its treasures.

In 4-6 days, you can visit the Colosseum, explore the Roman Forum, toss a coin into the Trevi Fountain, and enjoy authentic Italian cuisine. Add a day for a trip to Vatican City or Pompeii.
