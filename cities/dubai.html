How many days to spend in Dubai

Dubai, a city of opulence and innovation, offers a mix of modern wonders and cultural experiences. To fully experience Dubai, we recommend spending 4-6 days.

With 4-6 days, you can visit the Burj Khalifa, explore the Dubai Mall, take a desert safari, and relax on Jumeirah Beach. Add a day to visit the old town of Deira for a taste of traditional Dubai.

Suggested Itinerary

  • Day 1: Visit the Burj Khalifa and explore the Dubai Mall.
  • Day 2: Take a desert safari with dune bashing and dinner.
  • Day 3: Relax on Jumeirah Beach and visit the Palm Jumeirah.

Top attractions in Dubai

Burj Khalifa

Visit the world’s tallest building for breathtaking views.

Dubai Mall

Shop and explore the aquarium in this massive mall.

Palm Jumeirah

Discover this man-made island with luxury resorts.

Local tip

Visit the Dubai Fountain show in the evening for a mesmerizing display of water and lights.
