How Many Days to Spend in Dubai

Dubai, a city of towering skyscrapers and desert adventures, offers a mix of modernity and tradition. We recommend spending 4-6 days.

In 4-6 days, you can visit the Burj Khalifa, explore the Dubai Mall, take a desert safari, and stroll through the Gold Souk. Add a day for a trip to Abu Dhabi to see the Sheikh Zayed Grand Mosque.

Local Tip

Visit the Dubai Frame at sunset for a magical view of both old and new Dubai.
