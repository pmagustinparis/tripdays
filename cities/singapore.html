How Many Days to Spend in Singapore

Singapore, a modern city-state, blends futuristic architecture with lush greenery. We recommend spending 3-5 days to explore its highlights.

In 3-5 days, you can visit Marina Bay Sands, explore Gardens by the Bay, stroll through Chinatown, and enjoy Sentosa Island. Add a day for a food tour or a visit to the Night Safari.

Local Tip

Visit Gardens by the Bay at night to see the Supertree Grove light show.

Top Attractions in Singapore

  • Marina Bay Sands: Enjoy the infinity pool and views of the skyline.
  • Gardens by the Bay: Marvel at the Supertree Grove and Flower Dome.
  • Sentosa Island: Relax on the beach or visit Universal Studios.
