How Many Days to Spend in Istanbul

Istanbul, a city bridging two continents, is rich in history and culture. We recommend spending 4-6 days to explore its wonders.

In 4-6 days, you can visit the Hagia Sophia, explore the Grand Bazaar, cruise the Bosphorus, and wander through the Sultanahmet district. Add a day for a trip to the Asian side or the Prince Islands.

Local Tip

Take a ferry to the Prince Islands for a peaceful escape from the city.
