How many days to spend in Istanbul

Istanbul, a city where East meets West, offers a rich blend of history and culture. To fully experience Istanbul, we recommend spending 4-6 days.

With 4-6 days, you can visit the Hagia Sophia, explore the Grand Bazaar, take a Bosphorus cruise, and wander through the historic Sultanahmet district. Add a day to explore the Asian side for a complete experience.

Suggested Itinerary

  • Day 1: Visit the Hagia Sophia and Blue Mosque.
  • Day 2: Explore the Grand Bazaar and Spice Market.
  • Day 3: Take a Bosphorus cruise and visit Dolmabahçe Palace.
