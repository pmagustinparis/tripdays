How Many Days to Spend in New York

New York City, a global hub of culture and energy, has something for everyone. We recommend spending 5-7 days to explore its highlights.

In 5-7 days, you can visit the Statue of Liberty, explore Times Square, walk through Central Park, and catch a Broadway show. Add a day for a trip to Brooklyn or the Metropolitan Museum of Art.

Local Tip

Visit the High Line early in the morning to enjoy the views without the crowds.

Top Attractions in New York

  • Statue of Liberty: Take a ferry to this iconic symbol of freedom.
  • Times Square: Experience the vibrant energy of this famous square.
  • Central Park: Enjoy a walk or picnic in this urban oasis.
