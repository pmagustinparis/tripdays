How many days to spend in New York

New York City, with its iconic skyline and endless attractions, offers something for everyone. To fully experience NYC, we recommend spending 5-7 days.

With 5-7 days, you can visit the Statue of Liberty, explore Central Park, catch a Broadway show, and take in the views from the Empire State Building. Add a day to explore Brooklyn or the MET for a deeper dive.

Suggested Itinerary

  • Day 1: Visit the Statue of Liberty and explore Lower Manhattan.
  • Day 2: Walk through Central Park and visit Times Square.
  • Day 3: Explore the Metropolitan Museum of Art.
