How many days to spend in Tokyo

Tokyo, a city of contrasts, blends tradition with modernity. To fully experience its vibrant culture, we recommend spending 7-10 days.

With 7-10 days, you can visit Senso-ji Temple, explore Shibuya and Shinjuku, take a day trip to Mount Fuji, and immerse yourself in Tokyo’s unique neighborhoods like Akihabara and Harajuku.

Suggested Itinerary

  • Day 1: Experience Shibuya Crossing and visit Senso-ji Temple.
  • Day 2: Explore Tokyo Skytree and the Akihabara district.
  • Day 3: Take a day trip to Mount Fuji.
