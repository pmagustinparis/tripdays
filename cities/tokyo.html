How Many Days to Spend in Tokyo

Tokyo, a city where tradition meets innovation, offers a unique blend of experiences. We recommend spending 7-10 days to fully immerse yourself.

In 7-10 days, you can explore Shibuya Crossing, visit Senso-ji Temple, shop in Ginza, and experience a traditional tea ceremony. Add a day trip to Nikko or Hakone for a taste of Japan's natural beauty.

Local Tip

Explore the narrow alleys of Dotonbori in Shinjuku for hidden izakayas and local vibes.
