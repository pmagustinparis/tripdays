How many days to spend in London

London, a city steeped in history and culture, offers endless attractions. To fully experience London, we recommend spending 5-7 days.

With 5-7 days, you can visit the Tower of London, explore the British Museum, stroll through Hyde Park, and catch a West End show. Add a day trip to Stonehenge or Windsor Castle for a deeper dive into the region.

Suggested Itinerary

  • Day 1: Visit the Tower of London and Tower Bridge.
  • Day 2: Explore the British Museum and Covent Garden.
  • Day 3: Stroll through Hyde Park and see Buckingham Palace.

Top attractions in London

Tower of London

Discover the history of this iconic fortress and see the Crown Jewels.

British Museum

Explore a vast collection of world artifacts, including the Rosetta Stone.

Hyde Park

Relax in this sprawling park, perfect for a leisurely walk or picnic.

Local tip

Take a ride on the London Eye for stunning views of the city, especially at sunset.
