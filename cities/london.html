How Many Days to Spend in London

London, a city of history, culture, and modernity, offers endless attractions. To fully experience London, we recommend spending 5-7 days.

With 5-7 days, you can explore the Tower of London, visit the British Museum, stroll through Hyde Park, and catch a show in the West End. Add a day trip to Stonehenge or Windsor Castle for a deeper dive into British history.

Suggested Itinerary

  • Day 1: Visit the British Museum and stroll through Hyde Park.
  • Day 2: Explore the Tower of London and see the Crown Jewels.
  • Day 3: Catch a show in the West End and dine in Covent Garden.

Local Tip

Take a walk along the South Bank at sunset for stunning views of the city skyline.
