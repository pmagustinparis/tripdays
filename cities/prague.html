How Many Days to Spend in Prague

Prague, a city of fairy-tale charm, is filled with history and beauty. We recommend spending 3-5 days to explore its highlights.

In 3-5 days, you can visit Prague Castle, walk across Charles Bridge, explore Old Town Square, and enjoy a river cruise on the Vltava. Add a day for a trip to Český Krumlov.

Local Tip

Walk across Charles Bridge at sunrise for a magical, crowd-free experience.
