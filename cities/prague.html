How many days to spend in Prague

Prague, with its fairy-tale architecture and rich history, is a charming destination. To fully experience Prague, we recommend spending 3-5 days.

With 3-5 days, you can visit Prague Castle, stroll across Charles Bridge, explore Old Town Square, and enjoy the views from Petřín Hill. Add a day to visit Vyšehrad for a quieter, local experience.

Suggested Itinerary

  • Day 1: Visit Prague Castle and St. Vitus Cathedral.
  • Day 2: Stroll across Charles Bridge and explore Old Town Square.
  • Day 3: Climb Petřín Hill for panoramic views of the city.
