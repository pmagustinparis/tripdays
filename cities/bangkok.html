How many days to spend in Bangkok

Bangkok, a bustling metropolis, blends ancient temples with modern skyscrapers. To fully experience its vibrant culture, we recommend spending 5-7 days.

With 5-7 days, you can visit the Grand Palace, explore the floating markets, shop at Chatuchak Market, and take a day trip to Ayutthaya. This duration allows you to dive into the city’s rich history and lively street food scene.

Suggested Itinerary

  • Day 1: Visit the Grand Palace and Wat Arun.
  • Day 2: Explore Chatuchak Market and enjoy street food.
  • Day 3: Take a boat tour along the Chao Phraya River.

Top attractions in Bangkok

Grand Palace

Marvel at the stunning architecture and the Emerald Buddha.

Wat Arun

Visit the beautiful Temple of Dawn along the Chao Phraya River.

Chatuchak Market

Shop for souvenirs at this massive weekend market.

Local tip

Try street food at night markets like Talat Rot Fai for an authentic taste of Bangkok.
