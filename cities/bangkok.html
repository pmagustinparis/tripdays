How Many Days to Spend in Bangkok

Bangkok, a bustling metropolis, blends ancient temples with modern skyscrapers. To experience its essence, we recommend spending 3-5 days.

In 3-5 days, you can visit the Grand Palace, explore Wat Arun, shop at Chatuchak Market, and take a boat ride along the Chao Phraya River. Add a day for a floating market or a trip to Ayutthaya for a historical excursion.

Local Tip

Skip the touristy floating markets and visit Amphawa Market on the weekend for an authentic experience.

Top Attractions in Bangkok

  • Grand Palace: Marvel at the stunning architecture and the Emerald Buddha.
  • Wat Arun: Visit the beautiful Temple of Dawn along the Chao Phraya River.
  • Chatuchak Market: Shop for souvenirs at this massive weekend market.
