How many days to spend in Barcelona

Barcelona, a city of art, architecture, and beaches, offers a perfect Mediterranean escape. To fully experience Barcelona, we recommend spending 4-6 days.

With 4-6 days, you can visit the Sagrada Família, stroll down La Rambla, explore Park Güell, and relax on Barceloneta Beach. Add a day to visit Montserrat for a scenic day trip.

Suggested Itinerary

  • Day 1: Visit the Sagrada Família and Casa Batlló.
  • Day 2: Stroll down La Rambla and explore the Gothic Quarter.
  • Day 3: Discover Park Güell and relax on Barceloneta Beach.
