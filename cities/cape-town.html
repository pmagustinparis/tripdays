How Many Days to Spend in Cape Town

Cape Town, with its dramatic landscapes and rich history, is a gem. We recommend spending 4-6 days to explore its highlights.

In 4-6 days, you can hike Table Mountain, visit Robben Island, explore the V&A Waterfront, and drive along the Cape Peninsula. Add a day for a wine tour in Stellenbosch.

Local Tip

Hike up Table Mountain early in the morning to beat the heat and crowds.

Top Attractions in Cape Town

  • Table Mountain: Take a cable car to the top for breathtaking views.
  • Robben Island: Visit the historic prison where Nelson Mandela was held.
  • V&A Waterfront: Shop, dine, and enjoy the harbor views.
