How many days to spend in Cape Town

Cape Town, with its stunning landscapes and vibrant culture, is a must-visit destination. To fully experience Cape Town, we recommend spending 5-7 days.

With 5-7 days, you can hike Table Mountain, visit Robben Island, explore the Cape Winelands, and drive along the Cape Peninsula to see the penguins at Boulders Beach. This duration allows you to soak in the city’s natural beauty and history.

Suggested Itinerary

  • Day 1: Hike or take the cable car up Table Mountain.
  • Day 2: Visit Robben Island and explore the V&A Waterfront.
  • Day 3: Drive to Cape Point and see the penguins at Boulders Beach.

Top attractions in Cape Town

Table Mountain

Enjoy panoramic views from this iconic flat-topped mountain.

Robben Island

Learn about South Africa’s history at this UNESCO site.

Boulders Beach

Meet the adorable African penguins at this scenic beach.

Local tip

Visit the Cape Winelands for a day of wine tasting and stunning vineyard views.
