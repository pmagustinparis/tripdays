How Many Days to Spend in Rio de Janeiro

Rio de Janeiro, with its stunning beaches and lively culture, is a must-see. We recommend spending 4-6 days to experience its energy.

In 4-6 days, you can visit Christ the Redeemer, relax on Copacabana Beach, explore Sugarloaf Mountain, and wander through Santa Teresa. Add a day for a trip to Ilha Grande or a samba show.
