How many days to spend in Kyoto

Kyoto, the cultural heart of Japan, is known for its temples, gardens, and traditions. To fully experience Kyoto, we recommend spending 4-6 days.

With 4-6 days, you can visit Fushimi Inari Shrine, explore the Arashiyama Bamboo Grove, see Kinkaku-ji (Golden Pavilion), and stroll through the historic Gion district. Add a day to experience a traditional tea ceremony or visit nearby Nara.

Suggested Itinerary

  • Day 1: Visit Fushimi Inari Shrine and its thousands of torii gates.
  • Day 2: Explore the Arashiyama Bamboo Grove and nearby temples.
  • Day 3: See Kinkaku-ji and stroll through Gion.

Top attractions in Kyoto

Fushimi Inari Shrine

Walk through thousands of red torii gates at this iconic shrine.

Arashiyama Bamboo Grove

Stroll through this serene bamboo forest.

Kinkaku-ji (Golden Pavilion)

Admire this stunning gold-covered temple.

Local tip

Visit Gion in the evening to spot a geisha on her way to an appointment.
