How Many Days to Spend in Kyoto

Kyoto, a city of temples and traditions, offers a glimpse into Japan's past. We recommend spending 3-5 days to soak in its charm.

In 3-5 days, you can visit Fushimi Inari Shrine, explore Kinkaku-ji (Golden Pavilion), stroll through Arashiyama Bamboo Grove, and experience a tea ceremony. Add a day for a trip to Nara or Uji.
