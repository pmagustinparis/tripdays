How many days to spend in Paris

Paris, the City of Lights, is a dream destination with its iconic landmarks, charming streets, and world-class cuisine. To fully experience Paris, we recommend spending 4-6 days.

With 4-6 days, you can visit the Eiffel Tower, explore the Louvre Museum, stroll along the Champs-Élysées, and take a day trip to Versailles. This gives you enough time to soak in the city’s romance and culture without feeling rushed.

Suggested Itinerary

  • Day 1: Visit the Eiffel Tower and stroll along the Champs-Élysées.
  • Day 2: Explore the Louvre Museum and relax in the Tuileries Garden.
  • Day 3: Take a day trip to Versailles to see the palace and gardens.
