How many days to spend in Paris

Paris, the City of Lights, is a dream destination with its iconic landmarks, charming streets, and world-class cuisine. To fully experience Paris, we recommend spending 4–6 days.

With 4–6 days, you can visit the Eiffel Tower, explore the Louvre Museum, stroll along the Champs-Élysées, and take a day trip to Versailles. This gives you enough time to immerse yourself in the city’s culture and history.

Suggested Itinerary for Paris

Day 1: Arrival and Exploration

Arrive in Paris, check into your accommodation, and take a leisurely stroll along the Seine River. Visit Notre-Dame Cathedral and enjoy a coffee at a local café.

Day 2: Iconic Landmarks

Visit the Eiffel Tower in the morning, then head to the Arc de Triomphe and walk down the Champs-Élysées. End your day with a sunset cruise on the Seine.

Day 3: Art and Culture

Spend the day at the Louvre Museum, followed by a visit to the Musée d’Orsay. In the evening, explore the Montmartre district and visit the Sacré-Cœur Basilica.

Day 4: Day Trip to Versailles

Take a day trip to the Palace of Versailles. Explore the palace, gardens, and the Trianon estates. Return to Paris in the evening for dinner in the Latin Quarter.

Day 5: Hidden Gems

Discover hidden gems like the Marais district, visit the Picasso Museum, and enjoy a picnic in the Luxembourg Gardens. End your day with a visit to a local patisserie.

Day 6: Leisure and Departure

Spend your final day shopping at Galeries Lafayette, visiting the Palais Garnier, or relaxing in a park. Depart from Paris with unforgettable memories.
